Liberty

A Meditation
Among the tenants of Truth stands an angelic emissary. The goddess Liberty, adorned with a heptagonal diadem and garbed in flowing linen robes, brandishes an illuminating torch in her dexter hand, while by her side her left grasps a defensive blade. Her crowned wisdom blazes radiantly, sealing her celestial superiority. Behind imposing, tempestuous cumuli roil against a portentous firmament. Beneath a plaque inscribed:
All thought pondered. All value expressed. All Truth known. The sentinel Liberty secures prosperity for the vigilant. This hand, an enemy to tyrants, seeks with the sword peaceful repose under liberty.
